About me

My web development experience comes from my last position as a Junior Software Developer / Business Analyst, and from four modules in computer science that I took while studying at the University of Nottingham: Programming (C), Computer Systems Architecture (theory, and some ARM assembly), Object-Oriented Programming (Java), and Web Programming and Scripting (PHP, HTML, CSS, MySQL).

I have a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy from King's College London (with a grade of 2:1). My philosophy degree has taught me a number of transferrable skills, from reading dense material, to making critical analysis and formulating logically coherent arguments. I studied a wide range of philosophical topics, from ancient Greek philosophy to formal logic.
